Questions and answers
How can I get an increase in my FBA inventory storage limit?

Storage limits are reviewed monthly. If you have sold 8% or more of your inventory each week in the previous six (6) weeks, you are eligible for an increase in your storage limit.
When will I get paid?

Amazon Payments settles your account and initiates your first payment 14 days after your registration date.

On your settlement date, Amazon Payments transfers eligible funds to your bank account using an Automated Clearing House (ACH) or an electronic funds transfer. Once the transfer of funds is initiated, it can take 3-5 business days for funds to appear in your bank account, not including holidays or weekends.

Subsequently, Amazon Payments will automatically settle your account and transfer eligible funds to your bank account at 14 day intervals.

Learn more about when you will be paid.
How do I fix an incorrect bank account number?

Funds that cannot be transferred because the bank account information is incorrect will appear in the current balance of your seller account for reprocessing. Update your bank account information for future settlements or payments. Learn more.
What fees does Amazon deduct from my account balance?

You can view the fees that were deducted from your account balance on the Payments page in your seller account. Fees are automatically deducted from your account balance prior to the transfer of funds.

To view your fees:

  1. On the Reports link in your seller account, select Payments.
  2. On the Payments page, click Transaction View.
  3. Select Service Fees from the Filter view by drop-down menu, and click Update.

Learn more about the selling on Amazon fee schedule.
How do I get paid?

Amazon Payments transfers funds directly to the bank account you specify in your seller account via an Automated Clearing House (ACH) or electronic transfer. Note that Amazon Payments cannot make payments to your credit card or PayPal account and cannot issue funds in the form of an Amazon Gift Card.

For a disbursement to take place, you must have valid bank account and credit card information on file for your seller account.

Note: Add or update your account information in the Settings section of your seller account.

Learn more about account settings.
